Experience based recommendations.

Software Recommendations

The Operating System (hereinafter "OS") should be free of "Game Booster" type "enhancers"
The OS should work in "Balanced" power mode or dedicated to the processor while the OMSI is running.
OMSI should patch NTCore 4GB Patch

The NVIDIA graphics card should have the following settings for OMSI:
  • Image sharpening - As requested by the user.
  • Anisotropic Filtering - As requested by the user.
  • Antialiasing - FXAA - As requested by user.
  • Antialiasing - gamma correction - As desired.
  • Antialiasing - mode - As requested by the user. recommended setting: Disable; override application settings
  • Antialiasing - settings - As requested by the user.
  • Anti-aliasing - transparency - As requested by the user.
  • Maximum frame rate - Maximum monitor refresh rate
  • CUDA - Graphics Processors - As requested by the user.
  • Low latency mode - As requested by the user.
  • Multi-frame sampling anti-aliasing (MFAA) - Not tested
  • OpenGL rendering graphics card - Not applicable to OMSI
  • Power Management Mode - Prefer maximum performance.
  • Shader Cache - Enable
  • Texture Filtering -Sample Anisotropic Optimization - On
  • Texture filtering - negative LOD bias - Lock.
  • Texture Filtering - Quality - High Performance.
  • Texture Filtering -Trilinear Optimization - On
  • Threaded optimization - Off.
  • Triple buffering - Not applicable to OMSI
  • Vertical sync - Use 3D app setting
  • VIrtual Reality pre-rendered frames - Use setting 3D applications.
  • Environment closure - Not applicable to OMSI
Settings affecting simulation quality are underlined.

If your NVIDIA Control Panel also has settings other than those listed above, please let me know.

It is recommended that you configure OMSI with this guide to complete the setup.
